What Huney is

Not a chore chart. Not a blank task app. A home operating layer.

Most household tools make you build the system yourself. Huney starts with the shape of your home, then helps create, schedule, and share the work that normally lives in one person's head.

The point

Make the work visible without making anyone feel managed.

Huney is meant to feel warm, practical, and human. It gives the household a plan without turning home into a performance dashboard.

Why it exists

For the moment when the list in your head gets too heavy.

Home can be full of care, routines, errands, maintenance, and tiny obligations, but still lack one shared place where all of that work can live.

Huney helps the household see what needs attention, who can take it, and when it fits into the week.

Less carrying the household plan alone.

Fewer tense check-ins about what got done.

Less forgetting when maintenance last happened.

Who it is for

Households that need coordination, not another lecture.

Couples

For partners who want a clearer way to split home work.

Families

For homes managing kids, pets, meals, school weeks, and health routines.

Roommates

For shared homes that need rotating ownership and lightweight accountability.

Busy adults

For people who want home cared for without becoming a project manager.
