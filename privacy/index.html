Huney Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Overview

Huney helps households manage tasks, reminders, meals, recipes, calendars, car care, maintenance, and shared responsibilities. This policy explains what information Huney may collect, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Information You Provide

Huney may collect information you enter into the app, including household setup details, tasks, reminders, routines, meals, recipes, household member details, car care information, and support requests.

Calendar Access

If you choose to connect your calendar, Huney uses calendar access to help plan household tasks and meals around real-life events. Huney only uses calendar information to provide app features you choose to enable, such as scheduling routines or publishing meal plans.

Recipes

If you add a recipe manually or parse a recipe from a link, Huney uses that information to organize ingredients, instructions, timers, meal plans, and cooking mode.

Account and Sync

If you create an account or join a shared household, Huney may use cloud sync to keep household information available across devices and household members. Shared household members may be able to see tasks, meals, routines, and other information added to the shared household.

How Information Is Used

Huney uses information to provide and improve app features, including:

  • Creating a household task list from the intake quiz
  • Planning tasks, reminders, meals, and routines
  • Supporting shared household features
  • Publishing meals or routines to your calendar when you choose to do so
  • Responding to support requests
  • Maintaining app reliability and security

Data Sharing

Huney does not sell your personal information. Huney may use service providers to operate the app, provide account sync, send support or account-related messages, process purchases through Apple, or maintain app reliability.

Purchases

Premium purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple. Huney may receive information needed to confirm subscription status, but Apple manages payment details.

Your Choices

You can choose which information to add to Huney, whether to connect a calendar, and whether to use shared household features. You can also contact support to request help with account or household information.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or need help with your data, email huney.support@gmail.com.
